Ground Rules

  1. Create a stock pile of items that you actually want to eat.
  2. Create a budget that you adhere to, and make buying stockpile items part of your weekly shopping routine.
  3. Cycle old items out of your stockpile once a month by eating them. This will keep your stockpile fresh.
  4. Aim to have enough supplies to last you at least 2 months
  5. Keep your stock pile separate from the rest of your food.
  6. Don't over do it. Once you hit that 2 month supply, don't waste money on extra supplies.
